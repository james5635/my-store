**Inventory Management System** is a comprehensive C# Windows Forms desktop application for inventory management and business operations. This is a complete, professional-grade system suitable for small to mid-sized businesses managing suppliers, staff, products, imports, orders, and payments.

### **Core Features**

- **7 Main Forms/Modules:**
  - **Staffs Management** – Employee records, positions, salaries, attendance tracking
  - **Suppliers Management** – Supplier information, contact details, addresses
  - **Customers Management** – Customer records and contact information
  - **Products Management** – Product catalog with pricing (cost & selling price) and inventory tracking
  - **Import Details** – Incoming inventory tracking from suppliers
  - **Order Details** – Customer order management and processing
  - **Payments** – Payment processing and financial tracking

### **Technical Stack**

- **Language:** C# (.NET Framework/Core)
- **UI:** Windows Forms
- **Database:** SQL Server with comprehensive schema (10 normalized tables)
- **Architecture:** Connected to a fully relational database with proper constraints and foreign keys

### **Database**

- **Schema.sql** – Complete database structure with 100 sample records per table
- **9 Core Tables:** Suppliers, Staff, Customers, Products, Imports, Orders, Payments, ImportDetail, OrderDetail
- **Data Integrity:** Includes constraints, unique keys, check constraints, and cascade relationships
