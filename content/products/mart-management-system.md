**mart-management** is a comprehensive **C# desktop application** designed for retail store management. It provides a complete solution for managing all aspects of a retail business with an intuitive user interface.

### Key Features:
- **Inventory Management** - Track and manage product stock levels
- **Sales Management** - Process customer sales and transactions
- **Purchase Management** - Handle supplier purchases and orders
- **Product Catalog** - Manage product information and details
- **Customer Management** - Store and manage customer data
- **Supplier Management** - Maintain supplier information and relationships
- **Employee Management** - Manage staff information
- **Category Management** - Organize products by categories
- **Payment Processing** - Handle payment transactions
- **Purchase & Sale Details** - Detailed transaction tracking

### Technical Highlights:
- **Language**: C# (100%)
- **License**: MIT (freely distributable)
- **Database**: Entity Framework Core with SQL
- **Stack**: .NET-based, modern C# backend
- **Status**: Active development

### Use Case:
This is an ideal solution for small to medium-sized retail businesses, supermarkets, convenience stores, or any retail operation needing centralized management of inventory, sales, purchases, and customer relationships.

The project is production-ready with proper database migrations, clean architecture, and is open-source under MIT license—perfect for resale or customization to specific client needs.
